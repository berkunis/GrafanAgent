## Trigger

A user sends three or more teammate invitations within a 10-minute window. This is a strong viral signal — they're evangelising internally and the invitees need a great first impression.

## Audience cut

Any plan. Inviter account age: any. Invitees must be on a distinct-from-inviter email domain count of ≤ 1 (i.e. all on the inviter's company domain) to avoid accidental external shares.

## Message angle

Two separate touches. Inviter: thank them, offer one resource for rolling out internally (a 10-minute recorded walk-through keyed to their primary use case). Invitees: a welcome message explicitly naming the inviter, a link to the inviter's first dashboard, and a two-click path to create their own.

## Guardrails

Suppress if any invitee has previously unsubscribed from onboarding emails. Never expose the inviter's email address in the invitee message — use their display name.

## Success metric

Invitee first-session-within-72h rate. Secondary: inviter's account seat expansion within 14 days.
