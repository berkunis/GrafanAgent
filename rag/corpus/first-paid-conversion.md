## Trigger

An account transitions from free or trial to a paid plan for the first time. This is a celebration moment but also a high-risk window — the first 14 days after payment are when buyer's remorse and quiet-churn risk peak.

## Audience cut

Any plan transition from `free`/`trial` to `team` or `enterprise`. First paid conversion for that account id (check against billing history).

## Message angle

Welcome message from the Head of Customer Success (real person, real signature), not marketing. Include: three quick wins tailored to their stated use case, the direct Slack line to their assigned CSM, a calendar link for a 15-minute kickoff. Follow up at day 7 and day 14 if the calendar link is unclicked.

## Guardrails

Do not pitch upsells in the first 30 days. Do not request a review, testimonial, or case study until post day 60. If the account was closed-won through a partner, cc the partner contact.

## Success metric

Day-30 retention and CSAT. Secondary: kickoff call completion within 14 days.
