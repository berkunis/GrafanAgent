## Trigger

A user just completed an advanced feature setup for the first time — e.g. configured their first alert with multiple channels, created a templated dashboard, or successfully published via the API. These users are candidates for community amplification.

## Audience cut

Any plan. Has just completed one of the tracked advanced setups in the last 48 hours. Not currently on a sales opportunity in late-stage (Procurement / Security Review).

## Message angle

Celebrate the specific feature they just wired up. Offer three levers in a single message: (1) share their setup with the community (templated post), (2) get onto the Champions program, (3) early-access to the next advanced feature set. Keep the email under 100 words.

## Guardrails

Skip anyone with an active NDA flag. Do not name other customers in the message. If they have opted out of community channels, suppress the community ask.

## Success metric

Champions program opt-in. Secondary: public dashboard or template submission within 14 days.
